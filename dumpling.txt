Ingredients

2 cups flour

1 1/2 teaspoon salt

1 tblsp Baking powder

2 tblsp vegetable oil

1 cup luke warm water



Directions: 
Add dry ingredients together well. add oil and mix with a fork. add water to the consistency you like. Drop a spoonful of dumpling onto chicken or beef broth or stew and let steam for 4 minutes. ( add chopped parsley to flour for a more unique flavor)
